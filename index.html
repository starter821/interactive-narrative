Is Washington State Still Affordable?
Over the past several years in Washington State, the cost of living has risen significantly due to inflation, rising housing costs, supply chain disruptions, and broader economic shifts. Wages have also increased, but for many workers they have not kept pace with essential expenses. This has widened the gap between earnings and the cost of maintaining a basic standard of living. This article examines these trends by analyzing changes in inflation, living costs, and wages over time, and explores how these shifts have shaped the affordability and economic policy in Washington State.
